The Problem

Why blue carbon projects need a better registry system.

Blue Carbon Is Hard to Track

Mangroves, seagrass, and coastal plants capture large amounts of carbon, but current systems make it difficult to measure and track how much is stored.

Current Challenges

  • No unified registry for blue carbon projects
  • Risk of data tampering and misuse
  • Possibility of double-counted carbon credits
  • Slow, manual verification processes

Our Solution

A blockchain-based registry for trusted blue carbon credits.

Secure Data Storage

All project data is stored on a blockchain ledger, making it tamper-resistant and auditable by any stakeholder.

Smart Contracts

Smart contracts automatically validate incoming data and issue verified carbon credits based on predefined rules.

Tokenized Credits

Each carbon credit is represented as a unique digital token, preventing double counting and making trading seamless.

How It Works

From field data to verified digital carbon credits.

1

Data Collection

Carbon data is collected from IoT sensors, satellites, or field surveys in mangrove and seagrass ecosystems.

2

On-Chain Upload

Collected data is submitted to the registry and stored on the blockchain as immutable records.

3

Smart Verification

Smart contracts review the data against project baselines and methodologies to ensure quality.

4

Credit Issuance

Verified blue carbon credits are generated and converted into tradable digital tokens.

5

Marketplace & Reporting

Buyers purchase tokens on a marketplace, and organizations use dashboards & APIs for carbon reporting.

Business Model

FAQ

Quick answers to common questions.

Blue carbon refers to carbon captured and stored by coastal and marine ecosystems such as mangroves, salt marshes, and seagrass meadows.

Blockchain provides an immutable ledger that improves transparency, prevents tampering, and reduces double counting of credits.

Project developers, NGOs, governments, carbon credit buyers, and companies needing reliable carbon reporting can all use the registry.
